Research Paper Trail

  2. A Logical Calculus of the Ideas Immanent in Nervous Activity by Warren S. McCulloch and Walter Pitts(1943), Bulletin of Mathematical Biophysics, Vol. 5 pp. 115-133
  3. The Perceptron: A Probablistic Model for Information Storage and Organization in the Brain by F. RosenBlatt(1958), Psychological Review Vol. 65, No. 6
  4. Learning Representations by Back-propagating Errors by David E. Rumelhart, Geoffrey E. Hinton & Ronald J. Williams (1986)
  5. Deep Residual Learning for Image Recognition by Kaiming He, Xiangyu Zhang, Shaoqing Ren & Jian Sun of Microsoft Research (2015)
  6. Attention Is All You Need by Ashish Vaswani, Noam Shazeer, Niki Parmar, Jakob Uszkoreit, Llion Jones, Aidan N. Gomez, Lukasz Kaiser and Illia Polusukhin (2017)
